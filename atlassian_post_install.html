About Lekh Diagram

Lekh Diagram is a sketch recognition diagramming and whiteboard app. This has a smart mode which recognizes your rough drawings of shapes such as rectangle, circles, connection lines etc and convert them into regular shapes.

It is recommended to use touch screens to utilize the full potential of shape recognition technology of Lekh Diagram. However you can use mouse and keyboard on non touch screens and use drag drop instead of shape recognition. You can also create diagrams in our mobile app (iOS and Android) and import them into Confluence wiki.
